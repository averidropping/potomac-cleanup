Why a River Cleanup?

Cleanups are a great way to connect with nature and gain first-hand experience with the plastic pollution problem. A cleanup is simple: choose a location, take a pair of gloves and a garbage bag with you, and start collecting waste.

This is a concrete and effective action that anyone can take to foster a cleaner planet. It’s also a gateway to greater involvement in sustainable practices.
